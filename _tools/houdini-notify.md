## Houdini Notify

---

## Problem

Working in Houdini often involves long cooking nodes, simulations, or TOP networks.

This leads to constant context switching. Either you sit and wait, or you alt tab and risk missing when the cook finishes.

Both cases break focus and reduce efficiency.

---

## Solution

Houdini Notify is a lightweight utility that sends Windows toast notifications when a node finishes cooking.

It allows you to step away or focus on something else while still being aware of when your work is ready.

---

## Features

- Sends Windows 11 toast notifications on cook completion  
- Works with regular nodes and TOP networks  
- Optional sound notifications  
- Custom icon support  
- Supports both local and remote audio files  
- Clean handling of notification dismissal  
- Simple integration inside Houdini
